How to Play

Playing the Pokémon Puzzle game is as easy as throwing a Poké Ball!

Step 1: Look at the Shadow

On the game page, you will see a silhouette of a Pokémon. Stare at it carefully and try to remember its shape.

Step 2: Make Your Guess

Type the name of the Pokémon you think it is into the input box provided. Don't worry about capitalization!

Step 3: Check Your Answer

Click the "Guess!" button. The game will tell you if you are correct. If you are, the Pokémon will be revealed!
